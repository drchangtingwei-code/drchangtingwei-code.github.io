Thoracic Surgeon & Metabolic/Bariatric Surgeon

About

Clear information for shared decisions

Dr. Chang integrates imaging, medical history, operative considerations, nutrition, and long-term follow-up when discussing treatment options. His practice emphasizes clear communication so that patients and families can understand the purpose, limitations, and risks of each next step.
